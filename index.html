Neuro physio

After Surgery

 

After surgery we offer a structured rehabilitation plan to maximise the benefits of the surgery.

 

Physiotherapy sessions support you to regain normal movement, muscle strength & endurance. 

 

RECOVER

 

Physiotherapists can work with you to help you return to your normal levels of fitness and health after the effects of deconditioning from illness or lengthy periods of shielding.

FALLS & HEALTHY AGING

 

Falls are not normal or an expected part of aging.

We use evidence based measures to assess your muscle strength, balance, endurance & movement. We provide client focused exercise programmes based on your goals.
