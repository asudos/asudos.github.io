About us

We offer a wide range of evidence-based physiotherapy services; 5 days a week, Monday to Friday (including early mornings and evenings).

 

Our appointments are carried out by experienced CORU registered physiotherapists.

 

GQ Physio was founded to deliver top-quality patient care that meets your goals whatever they may be.

 

I have enjoyed over 15 years in the field of physiotherapy.

 

I commenced my physiotherapy training at the University of Limerick in 2002 and graduated with an honours degree in Physiotherapy in 2006. I began my career working in private practice alongside a hospital rotational position at Kerry General Hospital. I then moved to Otago in New Zealand to work in a specialist rehabilitation unit. Following this I moved to Brisbane in Australia where I worked as a physiotherapist and physiotherapy manager at a private practice for 2 years and in specialist neurological rehabilitation for 2 years. During this time I also completed qualifications in Clinical Pilates, trigger point dry needling and pain management as well as neurological speciality courses in Bobath techniques, running rehabilitation and vestibular rehabilitation. 

 

I moved into private practise in London in 2019 after six years in the NHS working in the community, specialising in early rehabilitation at home following hospital discharge. In 2021 I moved home to Dublin and set up my physiotherapy clinic and Pialtes studio.

 

Geraldine Quain Clinical Director
