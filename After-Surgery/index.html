AFTER SURGERY

After surgery we offer a structured rehabilitation plan to maximise the benefits of the surgery. Physiotherapy sessions support you to regain normal movement, muscle strength and endurance. This enables you to fully recover and get back to doing things that you enjoy.

 

To do this we:

  • Work with your surgeon to ensure your programme is suitable for you

  • Work with you to achieve your recovery goals and focus on your overall health and wellbeing

  • Ensure your personalised rehabilitation programme is tailored to meet your needs

  • Incorporate Pilates exercises into your rehabilitation exercise programme, to improve your core muscle strength and flexibility
