Back and neck pain

This is one of the most common problems that people present to physiotherapy for.

 

To manage this we:

  • Assess and treat the area affected using manual therapy techniques suited to the cause of the pain

  • Offer advice on pain relieving postures and techniques to maintain improvements from your physiotherapy sessions

  • Incorporate Pilates exercises into your rehabilitation exercise programme, to improve your core muscle strength and flexibility

  • Work with you to achieve your recovery goals and focus on your overall health and wellbeing
